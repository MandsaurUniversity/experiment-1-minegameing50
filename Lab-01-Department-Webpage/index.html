About the Department

The Department of Computer Applications was established in 2005 as part of the Faculty of Science and Technology at Mandsaur University, Mandsaur, Madhya Pradesh. Over the past two decades, the department has grown into one of the most sought-after departments in the university. The department is committed to providing quality education in computer science and information technology, preparing students for successful careers in the IT industry.

Vision and Mission

The vision of the department is to become a centre of excellence in computer applications education and research. Our mission is to nurture technically competent professionals who can contribute to the growth of the IT sector in India. We aim to provide a learning environment that encourages innovation, critical thinking, and practical problem-solving skills among our students.

Infrastructure

The department boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure including three fully-equipped computer laboratories with over 120 computers, high-speed internet connectivity, a dedicated seminar hall with projection facilities, and a departmental library with over 2,000 books and journals. All labs are air-conditioned and equipped with the latest software tools required for the curriculum.
